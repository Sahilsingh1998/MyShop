Refund & Return Policy

At Chandan Cycle Store, we value your satisfaction. Our Refund & Return Policy outlines how you can return products and request refunds.

  1. Eligibility for Returns

    Products must be unused, undamaged, and in their original packaging. Returns must be requested within 30 days of purchase. Proof of purchase is required.

  2. Non-Returnable Items

    Some products cannot be returned, including customized bicycles or accessories. These exceptions will be clearly stated on the product page.

  3. Return Process

    To return a product, please contact our customer support at singhsahil.8340@gmail.com with your order details. Our team will guide you through the return process and provide a return authorization.

  4. Refunds

    Refunds will be issued after we receive and inspect the returned item. Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 7–10 business days of approval.

  5. Exchanges

    If you wish to exchange a product for a different model or size, please follow the return process and indicate your preferred replacement. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

  6. Shipping Costs

    Shipping costs for returned items may be borne by the customer unless the return is due to a defective or incorrect product. Original shipping charges are non-refundable.

  7. Defective or Damaged Items

    If you receive a defective or damaged product, please contact our support team immediately. We will arrange for a replacement or refund at no extra cost.
