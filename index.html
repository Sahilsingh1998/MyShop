Serving Kursela Since 1993

Chandan Cycle Store began as a humble family endeavor over three decades ago. Our foundation was built on a simple premise: everyone deserves a reliable ride. From our roots in Kursela, Katihar, we have expanded our expertise while maintaining the warm, personal touch of a family-run business.

Today, we don't just sell cycles; we build lasting relationships. Our team of expert mechanics treats every repair with precision, ensuring the safety and satisfaction of our community.
